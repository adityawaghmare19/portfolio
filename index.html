Emoify : An Emotion-Based Music Recommendation System with Adaptive Training

🏢 International Journal of Scientific Research in Computer Science, Engineering and Information Technology

DIO : https://doi.org/10.32628/IJSRCSEIT ISSN : 2456-3307 Working project
  • EMOIFY is presented as an intelligent music recommender system that has been crafted to dynamically suggest music based on a user's actual current emotional state. The core of the system lies in its robust facial expression recognition capability, which uses a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) combined with OpenCV and Deep Neural Network (DNN) models to scan for emotions with high accuracy.
  • The trainer dashboard particularly crafted for its acquisition of new emotion data and retraining of the underpinning deep learning model ensures ongoing refinement and calibration of the predictive power of the system.
  • This adaptive learning process allows EMOIFY to delivering personalized, context-sensitive suggestions that evolve over time, dramatically enhancing the user experience.
Deep learning Flask MongoDB Facial recognition

AI-Augmented Digital Twin for Personalized Learning in Virtual Classrooms

🏢 International Journal of Scientific Research in Computer Science, Engineering and Information Technology

DOI: https://doi.org/10.70849/ijsci E-ISSN : 3049-0251
  • The research examines the dynamic shifts in academic learning, specifically addressing the incorporation of digital platforms such as YouTube and artificial intelligence tools (for instance, ChatGPT and Gemini) alongside conventional educational approaches in todays era.
  • the research also underscores enduring and substantial challenges, including insufficient structured learning, the proliferation of misinformation,and excessive reliance on technology.
  • Furthermore, the study considers student viewpoints on emerging innovations such as Augmented Reality (AR) and the Metaverse, which may offer potential solutions to the aforementioned issues. it also gives a signt into framework for educators and developers aimed at establishing more effective, dependable, and student-oriented virtual learning environments.
